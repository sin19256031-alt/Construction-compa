Case studies — recent projects

Real examples showing the brief, our approach and the result. Costs are indicative and exclude VAT unless stated.

Renovation • Wolverhampton • Completed: 10 weeks
Open-plan kitchen transformation

Brief: Remove dividing wall, reconfigure layout and install new kitchen units with island. Approach: Structural opening with RSJ, new wiring and plumbing, bespoke cabinetry. Result: Bright open-plan kitchen, improved flow and increased property value. Indicative cost: £18,500 (materials & labour).

Extension • Staffordshire • Completed: 14 weeks
Single-storey rear extension with bi-fold doors

Brief: Create a family dining area with large glazed doors. Approach: Foundations, insulated slab, structural lintels, new roof and bi-fold installation. Result: Spacious dining area with natural light. Indicative cost: £36,000 (planning & build).

Driveway • Perton • Completed: 5 days
Resin-bound driveway with drainage upgrade

Brief: Replace failing driveway and improve drainage. Approach: Remove old surface, install new sub-base, resin-bound finish and channel drain. Result: Durable, low-maintenance driveway. Indicative cost: £4,200 (materials & labour).

Bathroom renovation • Wolverhampton • Completed: 2 weeks
Modern bathroom with walk-in shower

Brief: Replace dated bathroom and resolve damp. Approach: Remove old fittings, treat damp, install new waterproofing, tiling and fixtures. Result: Contemporary, waterproof bathroom with improved ventilation. Indicative cost: £6,800.
